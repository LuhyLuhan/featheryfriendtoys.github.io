What We Use

We use bird-safe, eco-friendly materials like cork, untreated wood, paper straws, cardboard, natural twine, wooden beads, popsicle sticks, and shredded paper to ensure your feathery friends are entertained and safe.

Supporting Cheeky Beaks

We proudly donate toys to Cheeky Beaks Parrot Rescue. They’ve unboxed and tested our toys with their rescues — check them out on YouTube for a firsthand look!
